About us

Skeeter Beeter is a locally owned and operated company that provides the Lowcountry with the weapons they need to keep mosquitoes at bay.

We understand that fighting against ticks and mosquitoes is not just about losing the itch; it’s also a matter of safety. With mosquitoes being able to transmit more harmful viruses and diseases every year, the only way to keep your family healthy is by providing an invisible barrier to stop the sting.

Customer service and environment

We are commited to customer services and the environment

Our mosquito barrier spray is safe for animals and humans and effective. And we don’t just care about keeping the environment healthy; we care greatly about the health and safety of your family and loved ones. Whether you want to have us come spray for an event, or sign up for every three months, we guarantee you will see a difference in your quality of life. Combating stinging insects in the Lowcountry really does make a difference.
